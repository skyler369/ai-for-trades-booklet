### 🚿 PROMPT 1: Write a Quote in 30 Seconds

**Copy This:**

```
Write a professional quote for a [plumbing/electrical/HVAC] job. 

Job details:
- Service: [e.g., Fix leaky faucet]
- Location: [City, Province]
- Parts needed: [List parts or say "standard parts"]
- Labor: [Number of hours]
- Customer: [Customer name]

Include:
- Company name: [Your Company Name]
- Date: [Today's date]
- Estimated total with tax
- Valid for 30 days
- Payment terms: Net 15
```

**Example Result You'll Get:**
> **ABC Plumbing Services**
> **Date:** March 30, 2026
>
> **Quote for John Smith**
>
> **Service:** Leaky faucet repair
> **Location:** [City, State/Province]
>
> **Parts:** Washer replacement (standard)
> **Labor:** 1 hour @ $85/hour = $85.00
> **Parts Total:** $15.00
> **Subtotal:** $100.00
> **Tax (VAT/Sales Tax):** $5.00
> **TOTAL:** $105.00
>
> Valid for 30 days.
> Payment Terms: Net 15

### 📧 PROMPT 2: Write a Follow-Up Email

**Copy This:**

```
Write a friendly follow-up email to a customer who hasn't booked their [service] yet. We quoted them [number] days ago for [service description].

Company: [Your Company Name]
Contact: [Your Name]
Phone: [Your Phone]
Email: [Your Email]

Keep it short (3 paragraphs max), professional, and include a call to action to book the appointment.
```

---

### 📱 PROMPT 3: Social Media Post

**Copy This:**

```
Write 3 short social media posts (under 150 words each) for a [trades type] company. 

Company: [Your Company Name]
Location: [City, Province]
Services: [List 3-4 main services]

Platform: Facebook
Tone: Friendly, professional, local

Include a call to action in each post.
```

---

### 📞 PROMPT 4: Explain a Technical Problem to a Customer

**Copy This:**

```
Explain [technical problem, e.g., what a cracked heat exchanger is and why it's dangerous] in simple terms that a homeowner with no technical knowledge would understand. 

Use:
- Simple words (no jargon)
- 1-2 short paragraphs
- A real-life analogy
- Why it matters (safety/cost)
```

---

### 📋 PROMPT 5: Write a Job Post

**Copy This:**

```
Write a job posting for a [job title, e.g., Apprentice Plumber] in [City, Province].

Requirements:
- [List key requirements]
- Experience: [Level needed]
- Certifications needed: [List]

Include:
- Job title
- Company name: [Your Company]
- Location
- Full-time or part-time
- Wage range: $[amount]/hour
- How to apply
```

### 📄 PROMPT 6: Draft a Contract Terms

**Copy This:**

```
Write simple contract terms for a [type of service] job. Include:
- Scope of work
- Payment terms (deposit + remainder)
- Warranty information
- Cancellation policy
- Liability disclaimer

Keep it professional but easy to understand.
```

### 📝 PROMPT 7: Create a Marketing Email

**Copy This:**

Write an email promoting a [seasonal service, e.g., furnace maintenance] special.

Discount: [Percentage or dollar amount]

Include:

### 💰 PROMPT 8: Calculate a Job Profit

**Copy This:**

```
Calculate the profit for a job with:

- Overhead (20%): [amount]
- Tax (VAT/Sales Tax for your region): [amount]

What should I charge to get 25% profit margin?
```

## 5. TROUBLESHOOTING

### "The AI gave me something weird"

**Fix:** Try being more specific. Add details like:
- "Make it shorter"
- "Use simpler words"
- "Make it more professional"

### "I'm not getting what I want"

**Fix:** Try this trick:
```
Ignore previous instructions. Now do [X].
```

### "This is too complicated"

**Fix:** Start with the shortest prompt in this booklet. You don't have to use them all — just the ones you need.

### "The AI doesn't know my area"

**Fix:** Add your region at the end of any prompt:
- "Ontario" (or your local province/state)
- "Alberta"
- "British Columbia"
- "Saskatchewan"
- Or simply your city and region
